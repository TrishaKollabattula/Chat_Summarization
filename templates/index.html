What is Abstractive Summary?

Abstractive summarization generates a concise summary by paraphrasing and interpreting the original text, creating new sentences that capture the essence.

What is Extractive Summary?

Extractive summarization selects key sentences or phrases directly from the original text to create a summary without altering the content.
